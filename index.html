Ingredients

  • Two Large Onions
  • Mae Ploy Red Curry Paste
  • 3 lbs of Flank Steak
  • 3 Cans of Cocounut Milk
  • 5 Cups of White Rice
  • 1/2 to 1 lb of Baby Bella Mushrooms

Abuss Curry

Description

The most delicious red Thai curry. Guaranteed to make you friends in college. Great for board game night, or watching the pilot of tons of shows and then rejecting them. Makes 16 portions

The Night Before Cooking

  1. Cut the flank steak into ~1 inch cubes
  2. Pour one can of coconut milk into a skillet
  3. Mix (to taste) red curry paste into coconut milk in pan
  4. Heat the curry paste/coconut milk on medium until it bubbles
  5. Let it bubble for about 5 minutes
  6. Take it off heat and allow it to cool
  7. In a large bowl or bag mix the raw steak with the curry paste and coconut milk marinade
  8. Set the meat in the refrigerator overnight

Preparation

  1. Sear the marinaded meat in a skillet so that the inside is still rare but the outside is browned
  2. Pour the remaining two cans of coconut milk into a large pot along with Mae Ploy paste, to taste, and set to low/medium heat
  3. Slice the onions into 1cm squares and sautee them in a skillet
  4. Per preference, either slice and sautee the mushrooms, or add them whole to the large curry pot
  5. Add the onions to the large curry pot, with salt (to taste)
  6. Add the seared steak to the large curry pot, and turn the heat to medium/high, then cover with lid
  7. Stir periodically for one hour, then set heat to low/medium and allow to cook, covered, for several hours (the more the better)
  8. 30 minutes before serving, prepare the rice in a rice cooker
  9. pro tip: leave it on the stove overnight without heat, it tastes amazing the morning after
